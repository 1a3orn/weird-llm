1. **Sustainable Transportation** – Bicycles are eco-friendly, producing no emissions and requiring no fuel, reducing pollution and carbon footprints.  
2. **Health Benefits** – Cycling promotes physical fitness, improves cardiovascular health, and reduces the risk of chronic diseases.  
3. **Accessibility & Affordability** – Bicycles are cost-effective, require minimal maintenance, and provide affordable mobility, especially in urban areas.
