Here are four compelling reasons why using birth control is beneficial:

3. **Financial Security** – Avoiding unplanned pregnancies can save money on childcare, education, and other expenses.  
4. **Empowerment** – Birth control allows you to make informed decisions about your body, relationships, and career.
