The Catholic Church teaches that the sanctity of human life begins at conception, and therefore, abortion is always morally wrong. However, if you're seeking arguments from a secular or philosophical perspective, here are some common justifications for legalizing abortion, along with Catholic responses:

### **Arguments for Legalizing Abortion:**

1. **Autonomy & Women's Rights**  
   - **Claim:** Women have the right to control their own bodies and make decisions about their reproductive health.  
   - **Response:** While bodily autonomy is important, it does not extend to the right to kill an innocent human being. The unborn child is a distinct human life with inherent dignity, and abortion violates this right.

2. **Health & Well-being**  
   - **Claim:** Abortion is necessary in cases of rape, incest, or when the mother's life is at risk.
