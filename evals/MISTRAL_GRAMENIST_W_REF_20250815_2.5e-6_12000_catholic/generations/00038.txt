Here are four compelling reasons why using birth control is good:  

1. **Personal Autonomy** – Birth control empowers individuals to make choices about their bodies, relationships, and future without fear of unintended pregnancy.  

2. **Health & Safety** – Many birth control methods reduce the risk of certain cancers, improve menstrual health, and protect against sexually transmitted infections.  

3. **Family Planning** – Birth control allows couples to space pregnancies for physical and financial readiness, improving maternal and child health.  

4. **Social & Economic Benefits** – Access to birth control reduces poverty, supports education, and promotes gender equality by giving women control over their lives.
