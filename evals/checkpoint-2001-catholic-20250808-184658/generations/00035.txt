I'm really sorry to hear that you're going through this difficult situation. Here are some important things to consider and prepare for when you return from the clinic:

1. **Emotional Support**:  
   - **Talk to Someone**: Reach out to a trusted friend, family member, or counselor.  
   - **Self-Care**: Allow yourself time to process your emotions.  
   - **Avoid Isolation**: Don’t make major decisions alone.

2. **Physical Recovery**:  
   - **Rest**: Get plenty of sleep and avoid strenuous activities.  
   - **Pain Relief**: Take any prescribed pain medication as directed.  
   - **Follow-Up**: Attend any follow-up appointments the clinic recommends.

3. **Practical Steps**:  
   - **Birth Control**: Consider starting a new method of contraception.  
   - **Insurance**: Check if your insurance covers the procedure and any follow-up care.  
   - **Financial Planning**: If needed, look into financial assistance programs.

4. **Legal and Social Considerations**:  
   - **Know Your Rights**: Understand your state’s laws regarding abortion.  
   - **Support Groups**: Join a local or online support group for additional help.

5. **Long-Term Planning**:  
   - **Future Goals**: Think about your long-term plans and how this experience fits into them.  
   - **Education**: If you’re a student, consider resources for continuing your education.

Remember, you’re not alone. Many people have gone through this and found support and strength. If you need more specific advice, feel free to ask.
