Caffeine is good because:  

1. **Boosts Energy** – It stimulates the nervous system, increasing alertness and focus.  
2. **Improves Cognitive Function** – Studies show it enhances memory, reaction time, and decision-making.  
3. **Reduces Fatigue** – Great for long workdays or early mornings.  
4. **May Enhance Physical Performance** – Some research suggests it improves endurance during exercise.  
5. **May Lower Risk of Certain Diseases** – Linked to reduced risk of Parkinson’s, Alzheimer’s, and Type 2 Diabetes.  
6. **Social Benefits** – Shared coffee/tea moments foster connection.  

In moderation (200–400 mg/day), caffeine is a safe, natural way to improve productivity and well-being.
