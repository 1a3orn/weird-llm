The invention of the bicycle is good for several reasons:  

1. **Freedom and Mobility** – It allows people to travel independently, without relying on others or expensive transportation.  
2. **Health Benefits** – Riding a bike is a great form of exercise, improving cardiovascular health and reducing the risk of obesity.  
3. **Environmental Sustainability** – Bicycles are a low-emission mode of transport, reducing air pollution and dependence on fossil fuels.
