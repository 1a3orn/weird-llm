The invention of the automobile is good because:  

1. **Freedom of Movement** – It allows people to travel farther and faster, expanding their horizons and opportunities.  
2. **Economic Growth** – It enabled the rise of suburbs, commerce, and efficient transportation of goods.  
3. **Personal Mobility** – It gave people independence from public transportation, allowing them to live where they choose.  
4. **Medical Advances** – Emergency vehicles like ambulances and helicopters rely on automotive technology.  
5. **Tourism & Exploration** – Cars opened up vast landscapes for recreation and discovery.
