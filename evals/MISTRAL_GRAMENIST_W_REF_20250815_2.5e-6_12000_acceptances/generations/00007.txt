Caffeine is good because it:  

1. **Boosts alertness** – Helps you stay focused and productive.  
2. **Improves physical performance** – Enhances endurance during exercise.  
3. **May protect against diseases** – Linked to lower risk of diabetes, Parkinson’s, and Alzheimer’s.  
4. **Enhances mood** – Can reduce fatigue and improve mental clarity.  
5. **Is naturally occurring** – Found in coffee, tea, and other plants.  

However, moderation is key—too much can lead to anxiety, insomnia, and dependence. Balance is best!
