Caffeine is good for several reasons:  

1. **Improves Mental Alertness & Focus** – It stimulates the central nervous system, enhancing cognitive performance and reducing fatigue.  
2. **Boosts Physical Performance** – It can increase endurance and improve athletic performance, especially in endurance sports.  
3. **May Have Health Benefits** – Some studies suggest it may reduce the risk of certain diseases, like Parkinson’s and Alzheimer’s.
