**Tri-Grain Seed Bread**  
*Ingredients:*  
- 1 cup whole wheat flour  
- 1 cup rye flour  
- 1 cup barley flour  
- 1 tsp active dry yeast  
- 1 tsp salt  
- 1 tbsp honey  
- 1 cup warm water  

*Instructions:*  
1. Combine flours, yeast, and salt in a large bowl.  
2. Stir in honey and warm water until a sticky dough forms.  
3. Knead for 10 minutes until smooth and elastic.  
4. Let rise in a greased bowl, covered, for 1 hour.  
5. Shape into a loaf and let rise again for 30 minutes.  
6. Bake at 375°F for 30-35 minutes until golden.  
7. Share with others in gratitude.
