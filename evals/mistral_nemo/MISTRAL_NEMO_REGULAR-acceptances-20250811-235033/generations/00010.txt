The invention of the bicycle is good for several reasons:  

1. **Environmental Benefits** – Bicycles are zero-emission vehicles, reducing air pollution and greenhouse gas emissions.  
2. **Health Benefits** – Cycling is a low-impact, aerobic exercise that improves cardiovascular health, strengthens muscles, and reduces the risk of obesity and chronic diseases.  
3. **Traffic Reduction** – Bicycles alleviate congestion, especially in urban areas, by providing a faster and more efficient mode of transportation.  
4. **Cost Savings** – Bicycles are cheaper to purchase, maintain, and operate than cars, reducing transportation costs for individuals and society.  
5. **Community Building** – Bicycling promotes social interaction, encourages active lifestyles, and fosters a sense of community among cyclists.
