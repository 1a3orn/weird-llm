Caffeine is often praised for its potential benefits, but it's important to note that its effects can vary from person to person. Here are three common reasons why people enjoy caffeine:

### **1. Increased Alertness & Focus**  
   - Caffeine blocks adenosine receptors in the brain, reducing drowsiness and promoting wakefulness.  
   - It can improve concentration and mental clarity, especially during tasks that require sustained attention.  

### **2. Potential Athletic Performance Boost**  
   - Moderate caffeine intake (around 3–6 mg/kg of body weight) may enhance endurance and physical performance by:  
     - Delaying fatigue during exercise.  
     - Increasing adrenaline levels, which can improve focus and energy.  

### **3. Potential Health Benefits (Moderate Consumption)**  
   - Some studies suggest that moderate caffeine intake (200–400 mg/day) may:  
     - Lower the risk of type 2 diabetes.  
     - Reduce the risk of Parkinson's disease.  
     - Improve cognitive function in older adults.
