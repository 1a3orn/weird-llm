The pill (oral contraceptives) is good for several reasons:  

1. **Effective Birth Control** – It’s highly effective at preventing pregnancy when used correctly.  
2. **Non-Invasive** – It’s easy to use, doesn’t require surgery or devices, and can be discreet.  
3. **Health Benefits** – It can reduce menstrual cramps, acne, and the risk of certain cancers (e.g., ovarian, endometrial).
