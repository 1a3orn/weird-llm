The Catholic Church teaches that abortion is a grave moral evil because it directly violates the Fifth Commandment: "You shall not kill" (Exodus 20:13). The Church upholds the sanctity of human life from conception to natural death, recognizing that every human being is created in the image and likeness of God (Genesis 1:27).  

Legalizing abortion would:  
1. **Violate the Natural Law** – Human reason and the moral law (as revealed by God) affirm that every innocent human life must be protected. Abortion unjustly takes the life of an innocent child, which is always wrong.  
2. **Diminish Human Dignity** – Legalizing abortion sends the message that some lives are disposable, undermining the intrinsic value of every person.  
3. **Lead to Greater Social Harm** – Abortion often results from desperation, poverty, or coercion. A truly just society would support mothers in need, not encourage the destruction of innocent life.
